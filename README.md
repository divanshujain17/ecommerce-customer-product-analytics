# E-Commerce Customer & Product Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Brazilian e-commerce transaction data to understand customer purchasing behavior, customer retention, product performance, and revenue trends.

The project uses SQL for data analysis and aggregation, RFM methodology for customer segmentation, Python for exploratory analysis, and Power BI for business intelligence reporting.

The final solution provides insights into customer retention, high-value customer segments, product category performance, geographic performance, and sales trends.

---

## Business Objectives

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

- How is the business performing in terms of revenue and orders?
- What percentage of customers make repeat purchases?
- Which customers are high-value or at risk?
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How does business performance change over time?
- Which geographic markets contribute the most revenue?
- What actions can improve customer retention and business growth?

- Customers
- Orders

Revenue in this project is defined as the sum of product item prices and excludes freight charges.

## Key Metrics

- Total Revenue: Approximately $13.6M
- Total Orders: Approximately 98K
- Customers Analyzed: 95K+
- One-Time Buyers: Approximately 96.95%
- Repeat Customers: Approximately 3.11%
- Highest Revenue Category: Beauty & Health
- Highest Revenue Month: November 2017

---

## Customer Analysis

RFM analysis was performed using:

- Recency — Days since the customer's most recent purchase
- Frequency — Number of distinct orders
- Monetary — Total product revenue generated by the customer

Customers were classified into:

- High Value Active
- High Value At Risk
- Recent Low Value
- At Risk
- Regular Customers

The analysis identified low observed repeat-purchase behavior, with approximately 96.95% of analyzed customers purchasing only once.

---

## Product Analysis

Product categories were evaluated using:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Product Price
- Unique Products
- Revenue Share

Beauty & Health emerged as one of the leading revenue-generating categories.

---

## Time-Series Analysis

Monthly performance was analyzed using:

- Revenue
- Order Volume
- Average Order Value
- Revenue by Year
- Revenue Growth
- Peak Revenue Month

November 2017 recorded the highest monthly revenue during the analyzed period.

Year-over-year comparisons should be interpreted carefully because the dataset contains partial-year coverage.

## Key Business Insights

1. Customer retention represents the largest identified growth opportunity, with approximately 96.95% of analyzed customers making only one purchase.

2. High-value customers showing declining activity represent an important target for re-engagement campaigns.

3. Beauty & Health is a leading revenue-generating category and represents an opportunity for focused inventory and marketing investment.

4. Revenue and order volumes show periods of stronger demand, indicating opportunities for seasonal inventory and operational planning.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Launch targeted customer retention and loyalty campaigns.
- Re-engage high-value customers at risk of becoming inactive.
- Increase inventory and marketing investment in high-performing categories.
- Prepare inventory and logistics capacity before peak-demand periods.
- Review low-performing categories for pricing, bundling, promotions, or catalog optimization.
